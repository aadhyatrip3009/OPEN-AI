CardioPredict: AI Doctor for Cardiovascular Assessment is an intelligent, AI-driven healthcare solution aimed at transforming the early detection and prevention of cardiovascular diseases, which remain one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. The system leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze a wide range of patient data—including demographic details (age, gender), clinical parameters (blood pressure, cholesterol levels, ECG readings), and lifestyle factors (physical activity, smoking habits, diet)—to accurately predict an individual’s risk of developing heart disease. By delivering real-time risk assessments, intuitive health reports, and personalized preventive recommendations, CardioPredict empowers users to take proactive control of their heart health without requiring immediate access to specialized medical professionals. Its user-friendly interface ensures accessibility for both patients and healthcare providers, making it especially valuable in rural and underserved regions where medical resources are limited. Additionally, the platform has the potential to integrate with wearable devices for continuous monitoring, enabling dynamic risk tracking and timely alerts. By combining scalability, accuracy, and accessibility, CardioPredict aims to reduce diagnostic delays, support clinical decision-making, and ultimately lower the global burden of cardiovascular diseases through early intervention and informed lifestyle changes.
